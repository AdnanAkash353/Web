Thumbnail Design

I design scroll-stopping thumbnails that boost click-through rates and make your content stand out in crowded feeds. With a mix of bold visuals, clear messaging, and strategic layout, I turn your video’s core idea into a thumbnail that instantly grabs attention.

Whether it's for YouTube, Shorts, Reels, or any social media platform, each thumbnail is crafted with high-impact design principles—balancing vibrant imagery, expressive typography, and audience psychology to drive more clicks.

From clean and branded to bold and dramatic, I tailor each thumbnail to match your content style and audience preferences—ensuring your video gets noticed and watched.

Services Process

A powerful thumbnail isn’t just about looking good—it’s about performance.
Here’s how I create them:

  • Reviewing your video content to capture the most clickable concept.
  • Researching trends and competitor thumbnails in your niche.
  • Designing with bold titles, expressive faces, and eye-catching composition.
  • Using color theory, contrast, and spacing for maximum visibility.
  • Matching the visual tone to your brand identity.
  • Providing revisions based on performance insights or personal feedback.

Product Animation

I create sleek and immersive product animations that bring your digital and physical cards to life—perfect for showcasing 3D business cards, VFC (Virtual Flash Cards), NFC cards, and other smart business tools with elegance and impact.

These animations highlight every detail—textures, reflections, transitions, and interactive elements—giving your audience a premium, realistic preview of your product. Whether it’s a stylish spin, smooth fold-out, or dynamic swipe-through showcase, I craft each motion to reflect professionalism and innovation.

Perfect for product launches, promotional reels, portfolio showcases, or social media ads—these animations instantly elevate your brand’s perceived value and help your product stand out in a crowded digital space.

Services Process

Polished animations start with thoughtful design and precise motion. Here's how I bring your card product to life:

  • Understanding your brand, product features, and target audience
  • Designing the 3D model of your card (business, VFC, or NFC)
  • Creating motion sequences to showcase functionality, design, and effects
  • Pioneering the Design World's FirstApplying realistic lighting, textures, and reflections for a premium look
  • Rendering smooth, high-resolution animations in preferred formats
  • Reviewing with you and refining the animation to your vision
